Bulls and Cows Number Guessing Game

How to Play

The computer will generate a four-digit random number, using the digits 1-9, with no repeated digits. Use these hints to help you guess the number:

  • Your guess must be 4 unique digits between 1 and 9.
  • Every correct digit earns a cow.
  • Every correct digit in the correct location earns a bull.
  • Get 4 bulls to win.
